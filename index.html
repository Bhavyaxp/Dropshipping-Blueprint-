Dropshipping Blueprint

1. Niche Selection

  • Choose a profitable and trending niche.
  • Ensure demand and manageable competition.
  • Use tools like Google Trends and AliExpress.

2. Supplier Selection

  • Find reliable suppliers (AliExpress, CJ Dropshipping, etc.).
  • Check reviews and delivery times.
  • Communicate with suppliers for reliability.

3. Store Setup

  • Choose a platform: Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.
  • Get a domain name and brand your store.
  • Design a user-friendly website.

4. Product Listing

  • Select winning products with good margins.
  • Write compelling product descriptions.
  • Use high-quality images and videos.

5. Marketing Strategy

  • Use Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, and Google Ads.
  • Leverage TikTok and influencer marketing.
  • Implement SEO and email marketing.

6. Order Fulfillment & Automation

  • Automate order processing with DSers or Oberlo.
  • Ensure smooth tracking and customer updates.

7. Customer Support & Branding

  • Provide quick responses to inquiries.
  • Handle refunds and complaints professionally.
  • Build brand trust for long-term success.
